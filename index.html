ABOUT COLLEGE

University College of Engineering Kanchipuram (UCEk) is one of the constituent colleges of Anna University Chennai. It was established in the year 2010. Our Mission is to impart sound engineering knowledge, skills and moral values needed for highly challenging dynamic and global careers.

IdeaHub

Generally a presentation conveys the information from a speaker to an audience.
Typically a demostration, introduction, lecture or speech on some particular topic.
Here, the participants either as a team or as an individual is required to make a paper
about a given topic or area, and then present it in front of the juries.

RULES

1. A maximum of 2 participants or individual participant per paper is allowed
2. Team Leader should create team after completing their registration and can invite their team member
3. No participants can be part of more than one team.
4. Only registered participants are allowed to participate in the event.
5. Each Team will get 10 minutes to present their paper and 5 minutes will be for queries.
6. A panel of judges will go through the papers and if you're shortlisted to give the presentation, you'll be notified.
7. The decision of the judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
8. The Submission of the soft copies can be submitted through Mail: sparkucek@gmail.com
9. The mail must be with the subject "IdeaHub".
10. For any queries contact student co-ordinators.

put in a custom room and a match is conducted to decide the winners and runner.

2. Team formation is strictly not allowed .
3. No cheating, no AFKs.
