PlayZone

Garena Free Fire is a multiplayer battle royale game. All participants are put in a custom room and a match is conducted to decide the winners and runner-up.



RULES

1.Each team must have 4 players (squad format). 2. Only your own mobile phones are allowed, no emulators.

3. Players must use the FREE FIRE Username provided in the registration form for the tournament.

4.Any change in username should be reported to support at least 4 hours before the match time.

5. Players are not allowed to use any 3rd party applications that will enhance the default gameplay.

6. Avoid using exploits/in-game that could potentially spoil the game environment.

7. A spectator will always be present to observe the game.

8.For any queries, contact student co-ordinators.
