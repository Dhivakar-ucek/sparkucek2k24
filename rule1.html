IdeaHub

Generally a presentation conveys the information from a speaker to an audience. Typically a demonstration, introduction, lecture or speech on some particular topic. Here, the participants either as a team or as an individual is required to make a paper about a given topic or area, then present it in front of the juries.



RULES

1. A maximum of 2 participants or individual participant per paper is allowed.

2. Only registered participants are allowed to participate in the event.

3. Each Team will get 10 minutes to present their paper and 5 minutes will be for queries.

4. A panel of judges will go through the papers and if you're shortlisted to give the presentation, you'll be notified.

5. The decision of the judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

6. The Submission of the Abstract can be submitted through Mail: sparkucek@gmail.com before 03 Nov 2024.

7. The mail must be with the subject "IdeaHub".
