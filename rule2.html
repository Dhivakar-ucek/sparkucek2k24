CodeFix

The process of identifying and removing errors from the given code. Here, the participants will be engaged in the process of fixing a bug which means they should identify, analyze and remove the error within the given time.



RULES

1. A individual participant is allowed. **Duration: 1 hour ** .

2. Participants will be provided with a set of programs containing bugs, which must be identified and fixed within the time limit.

3. The competition will cover a predefined set of programming languages C, SQL, Java, and Python.

4. The total number of questions for each participant is 5.

5. Only registered participants are allowed to participate in
the event.

6. Any attempt to manipulate the system, cheat, or tamper with the competition platform will result in disqualification.

7.The decision of the judges is final, and no arguments or appeal will be happen.

8. For any queries, contact student co-ordinators.
